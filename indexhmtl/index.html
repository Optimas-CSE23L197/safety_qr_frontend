About QR Sathi

Built for India's Schools.
Designed to Save Lives.

QR Sathi is a smart student emergency identity system. We give every school-going child a QR-enabled safety card that any bystander can scan to access critical emergency information — no app, no account, just a phone camera.

We're also building a full ecosystem: a School Admin Dashboard for managing student records, and a Parent Mobile App for real-time notifications and profile management.

Physical QR Smart Card
Durable, tamper-proof, weather-resistant card with student emergency profile encoded in QR — carried every day, trusted in every emergency.
Parent Mobile Application
Real-time alerts, profile management, scan history, and direct communication — all in a clean, intuitive parent app for iOS and Android.
School Admin Dashboard
Manage all student profiles, generate QR cards in bulk, track scan activity, update emergency contacts, and monitor safety records from one portal.

Everything you need to know before enrolling your school with QR Sathi.

Ask a Question
Does scanning require an app or internet?
No app is required. The QR code opens directly in any phone browser when scanned using the camera app. Basic emergency info (name, blood group, contact number) is embedded directly in the QR and works even without internet. Full profile access requires internet.
How long does it take to set up QR Sathi for our school?
Schools are onboarded within 24-48 hours. After signing up, you upload student data via CSV or our online form, we generate all QR cards, and dispatch them to your school. End-to-end setup including card delivery typically takes 5-7 working days.
Is student data safe and private?
Absolutely. All student data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, stored on ISO 27001 certified servers, and fully compliant with India's DPDP Act. Emergency-public data (name, blood group, contact number) is kept strictly separate from full medical data, which requires authorized login to access.
What if a student loses their QR card?
Lost cards can be deactivated instantly from the school dashboard. A replacement card can be ordered — Basic plan customers pay ₹49 per replacement; Premium plan includes 2 free replacements per student annually. New cards are dispatched within 3 business days.
Can parents update emergency information themselves?
Yes! Parents using our mobile app can update contact numbers, medical alerts, and emergency contacts at any time. The QR card automatically reflects updated information without needing a new physical card — the QR links to a live profile.
What types of schools can use QR Sathi?
QR Sathi works for any school — private, government, CBSE, ICSE, state board, international schools, colleges, and coaching institutes. We have custom plans for government districts looking to onboard multiple schools at once under a single umbrella account.
